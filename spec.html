Minimum Truth Record (MTR)

An open standard for accountable, auditable AI decisions

The Minimum Truth Record (MTR) defines the minimal set of information required to audit, validate, and trust AI-driven decisions in high-stakes environments.

As AI systems are increasingly used in finance, healthcare, compliance, hiring, and infrastructure, organizations must be able to answer basic accountability questions after a decision is made‚Äîwithout storing raw prompts, personal data, or model internals.

Design Principles

  • Minimum and purpose-built
  • Standardized and model-agnostic
  • Privacy-preserving by design
  • Practical to deploy in existing systems

Specification

The MTR specification is published as an open standard proposal.
